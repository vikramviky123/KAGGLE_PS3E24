Smoker or Not Prediction

Welcome to Prediction if a person is a Smoker or Not

The dataset for this exercise is a smoking prediction dataset, and our goal is to predict the target variable `smoking` based on various components and characteristics of individuals.

Context:

This dataset encompasses information about different health and lifestyle aspects of individuals, including factors like age, height, weight, cholesterol levels, and more. Our objective is to predict whether a person is a smoker or not based on these features.

The dataset includes the following independent variables:

Independent Variables:

  • age    : Age of the individual.
  • height(cm)    : Height of the individual in centimeters.
  • weight(kg)    : Weight of the individual in kilograms.
  • waist(cm)    : Waist size of the individual in centimeters.
  • eyesight(left)    : Eyesight in the left eye.
  • eyesight(right)    : Eyesight in the right eye.
  • hearing(left)    : Hearing ability in the left ear.
  • hearing(right)    : Hearing ability in the right ear.
  • systolic    : Systolic blood pressure.
  • relaxation    : Relaxation blood pressure.
  • fasting blood sugar    : Fasting blood sugar level.
  • Cholesterol    : Cholesterol level.
  • triglyceride    : Triglyceride level.
  • HDL    : HDL cholesterol level.
  • LDL    : LDL cholesterol level.
  • hemoglobin    : Hemoglobin level.
  • Urine protein    : Presence of urine protein.
  • serum creatinine    : Serum creatinine level.
  • AST    : AST (aspartate aminotransferase) level.
  • ALT    : ALT (alanine aminotransferase) level.
  • Gtp    : Gtp level.
  • dental caries    : Presence of dental caries.
  • smoking    : Target variable indicating whether the individual is a smoker or not.

Metrics:

You can evaluate the model using the Roc Auc Score (Roc_Auc) metric.
