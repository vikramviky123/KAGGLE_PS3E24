Model Analysis

1. Ensemble of 4 Models for Training

Training involves the use of four different models for Classification:

  • Random Forest Classifier
  • Historical Gradient Boosting Machine Classifier
  • XGBoost Classifier
  • LightGBM Classifier

2. Model Training

Model training is performed using k-fold cross-validation with 5 splits. The average of predicted values is computed to evaluate the model's performance.

3. Metrics Chosen

The following metrics are chosen to assess model performance:

  • Accuracy
  • Precision
  • Recall
  • F1-score
  • ROC-AUC
